Is this really free? ▼
Yes. Every course, every module, every video is completely free. No credit card required. No trial period. We believe every artist deserves access to this knowledge.
Who is this for? ▼
Any musician or artist who wants to build a real business around their craft. Whether you're just starting out or you've been at it for years, these courses cover the business foundations the industry doesn't teach you.
How are the courses delivered? ▼
Every module is a high-quality video walkthrough hosted on HeyGen. You can watch on desktop, tablet, or phone. Each course breaks down into 5-8 focused modules you can complete in under 15 minutes.
Do I keep access forever? ▼
Yes. Once you have access, you keep it. We'll never paywall content that's already free. Future courses may also be added to the free library.
I'm not based in the US — does this still apply? ▼
While some topics (taxes, LLCs, EINs) focus on US systems, the principles of branding, networking, licensing, distribution, and building a music business apply globally.
